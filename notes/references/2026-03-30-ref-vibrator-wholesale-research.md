### 4. BLE Protocol Assessment

**Lovense protocol** (well-documented, reverse-engineered):
- Uses BLE GATT with UART-like RX/TX characteristics
- Simple ASCII commands: `Vibrate:5;` sets intensity (0-20 scale)
- Three generations of UUIDs (all documented)
- Gen 2 uses Nordic UART Service UUID: `6e400001-b5a3-f393-e0a9-e50e24dcca9e`
- Fully supported by buttplug.io/Intiface

**White-label BLE protocols**:
- Most Chinese manufacturers use one of two approaches:
  1. **Standard Nordic UART (NUS)**: Same GATT UART profile as Lovense Gen 2. Common on quality devices.
  2. **Proprietary protocols**: Cheaper devices use custom BLE characteristics. Require manufacturer documentation or reverse engineering.
- WINYI and similar quality manufacturers provide their own app + SDK. Whether they expose raw BLE commands varies by manufacturer.

**buttplug.io compatibility** (IoST Index at iostindex.com):
- Supported brands: Lovense (all), We-Vibe, Kiiroo, Magic Motion, Mysteryvibe, OhMiBod, Vibratissimo, and many more
- Most quality BLE vibrators are supported or can be added
- Generic Chinese devices: NOT automatically supported unless they use a known protocol

**Vibease** (notable): Uses BLE 4.0 with proprietary encrypted protocol. Reverse-engineered (GitHub: eldstal/vibease). Scrambled + base64 + fragmented messages. More complex to integrate than Lovense.

### 5. Can You Get Open BLE Protocol for <$30/unit at 500qty?

**YES, with caveats.**

## VERDICT

### K33: Lovense Wholesale -- PARTIALLY VIABLE

- Lovense wholesale through Nalpac is **achievable but unverified on pricing**. Industry data strongly suggests $45-55/unit for Ferri at retailer quantities.
- At $50/unit, the haptic bundle clears 30% margin at both $149 (55.6%) and $199 (65.8%) price points.
- **A literary fiction project is unlikely to qualify through standard Nalpac channels** without an established retail presence. A direct Lovense partnership (leveraging the ChatGPT Pleasure Companion precedent) is the better path but requires business development outreach.
- **Kill test status: UNVERIFIED. Next step: email Lovense business development and Nalpac sales for actual pricing.**

### K34: White-Label BLE Alternatives -- VIABLE

- Quality white-label BLE vibrators meeting the minimum spec (compact, wearable, BLE, USB-C, body-safe silicone, 1hr+ battery) are available at **$10-18/unit at 500qty** from manufacturers like WINYI.
- At $15/unit, the haptic bundle achieves **75.7% margin at $149** and **80.9% at $199** -- far exceeding the 30% target.
- **The critical risk is not cost but quality control and app development.** A white-label device with no established app requires dezibel to build or commission a BLE control app.
- **BLE protocol compatibility is solvable**: specify Nordic UART Service in the OEM agreement, or integrate via buttplug.io for broad device support.
- **Kill test status: VIABLE with conditions. The 30% margin requirement is easily met. The open questions are quality assurance process, app development cost, and brand trust.**

### Recommended Path

1. **Short-term (launch)**: Use Lovense SDK integration -- let users buy their own Lovense device, dezibel provides the sync app. Zero device COGS, 92%+ margin on content. Reduces haptic tier to a feature, not a product bundle.

3. **Avoid**: Buying Lovense wholesale to resell. The margin is adequate but you're paying for a brand premium you don't need in a bundle, and you're dependent on a supply chain you don't control.
