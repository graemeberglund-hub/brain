## 5. Compliance / Regulatory

### SHAFT Compliance (SMS)
- **SHAFT = Sex, Hate, Alcohol, Firearms, Tobacco** — CTIA/carrier content restrictions for SMS
- Sexual content is **federally legal but restricted** in SMS marketing
- **Age-gating required**: Modal pop-ups or welcome-text birthday verification (MM/DD/YYYY)
- **Most major SMS platforms ban SHAFT content entirely**: Klaviyo allows age-gating only for alcohol/firearms, NOT sex. Twilio takes strict compliance approach.
- **Voyage SMS explicitly supports sexual wellness brands** with compliant age-gating
- Violations: up to **$1,000 per message** in fines; service suspension

### Apple App Store
- Apps with "pornographic material" (explicit descriptions intended to stimulate erotic feelings) are rejected
- **Dipsea/Quinn workaround**: Frame as **"sexual wellness"** not pornography. Dipsea founder: "We wanted people to feel like they were making a choice that was good for them." This framing got both apps approved with 18+ ratings.
- Content ratings: "Frequent Mature or Suggestive Themes" + "Sexual Content or Nudity" designations
- Key: audio erotica ≠ visual pornography in Apple's enforcement practice

### Lovense Content Moderation
- **UNVERIFIED.** No public App Gallery content moderation policy found. Their developer docs focus on technical integration, not content guidelines.

## Key Findings for Dezibel Integration

1. **No one has done story + haptic as a commercial product.** First-mover advantage is real. The Lovense ChatGPT Companion proves the tech pathway; Dame's Toy Stories proves the bundling concept. Nobody has combined both.

2. **Quinn's demographics are promising but age-skewed.** 56% of Quinn users are 18-24; dezibel's target may be older. Dipsea had broader age distribution before acquisition.

3. **The App Store path is proven.** "Sexual wellness" framing works. Dipsea and Quinn both operate in iOS with mature content ratings.

4. **SMS is a compliance minefield.** Most platforms ban sex content. Voyage SMS is the identified compliant option. Age-gating is mandatory.

5. **Wholesale pricing is the revenue model's critical unknown.** The entire margin structure depends on what Lovense (or an alternative device partner) charges at volume. This is the #1 item to validate before committing to the bundle model.

6. **Meibi is small but strategically positioned** for LATAM distribution if dezibel expands beyond North America. Their education + commerce model aligns with dezibel's positioning.
