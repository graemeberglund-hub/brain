## Thesis

The product is a native iMessage experience that cannot exist on any other platform — it uses read receipts, typing indicators, rich media, and haptic integration that are unique to Apple's messaging infrastructure. This positions dezibel as a cultural moment for iMessage, not just a product that happens to use SMS. Apple has the infrastructure (Messages for Business, iMessage Apps, App Clips), the precedent (content partnerships with Spielberg, Oprah, U2), and the incentive (platform differentiation vs. Android). The pitch: dezibel makes iMessage look like the future of storytelling.
