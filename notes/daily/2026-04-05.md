### Dezibel: Micro-Participation Research (dezibel-editor)

### Dezibel: Cohort Community Ecology (dezibel-editor)

Designed a cohort-gated reader community system — "The Living Room." Each weekly cohort gets a discussion space with river-lock architecture (rooms open per week, close behind you, structurally preventing spoilers). Researched 25+ platforms across art-forward communities (Are.na, Glass, StoryGraph), cohort precedents (Maven 96% retention, ARGs, Reforge), and moderation approaches. Kill-tested the ecology concept: character accounts posing as real people were CRITICAL risk (legal, parasocial, believability expiration) — removed entirely. Final design: authored artifacts (photos, scraps, video fragments) placed by the author, not by fictional characters. Room is free alongside paid story, seeding sequel anticipation through unanswered questions. Scale corrected from "50 readers per cohort" to thousands per week with sub-room architecture (~150-200 per room within larger cohorts) — the system must be designed for explosive growth from day one.
