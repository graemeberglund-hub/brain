## Thesis

Fiction can help people by modeling ways of living — not through instruction but through example embedded in story. Dezibel's real-time intimacy format (iMessage, 42 days) amplifies this: readers don't observe the story, they live alongside it. The positive examples arrive through character behavior, not morals.
