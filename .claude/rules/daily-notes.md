When editing daily notes:
- Never add an `updated:` field — daily notes do not have one
- Maintain section order: Work, Decisions, Captured, Notes
- Append to existing sections rather than overwriting
- Use `[[wikilink]]` syntax for cross-references to other notes
- Keep entries concise — daily notes are operational logs, not essays
