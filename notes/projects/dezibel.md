## About

Dezibel is a serialized novel delivered over iMessage/SMS across 42 days (6 weeks, Sunday to Sunday). Readers receive daily text conversations between Hasta and Emma, with an embedded erotic novella (Poppi Devours Fanzo), audio elements, and an optional Lovense haptic tier. The governing phrase is "ascent as collapse" — direction upward, form falling apart.

**Current state:** 70% through writing. Functional Day 1 demo (Next.js/Firebase/Twilio/Vercel). No raise completed. No lawyer. Cast identified via relationships, not contracts.
